The PickUp Store

PickUp is a food restaurant that has been serving the community for years. Our passion is to bring people together over a meal and create memorable dining experiences. We believe in using only the freshest ingredients to create our dishes and take pride in serving high-quality food. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be happy to assist you with menu recommendations and answer any questions you may have.
