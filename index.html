The PickUp Store

PickUp is a food restaurant that has been serving the community for years. Our passion is to bring people together over a meal and create memorable dining experiences. We believe in using only the freshest ingredients to create our dishes and take pride in serving high-quality food. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be happy to assist you with menu recommendations and answer any questions you may have.

Coffee

Coffee lovers, look no further! At PickUp, we take our coffee seriously and strive to serve the best cup in town. Our coffee beans are carefully sourced from the finest regions around the world and roasted to perfection.

Drinks

At PickUp, we understand that sometimes you need a little something extra to quench your thirst. That's why we offer a range of refreshing drinks to complement your meal or to enjoy on their own.

Burger

Burgers, oh how we love thee! At PickUP, we believe that a great burger is the epitome of comfort food, and we're dedicated to making the best burgers in town. Our burgers are made with high-quality and served on a toasted bun.

Pizza

Pizza, the ultimate comfort food! At PickUP, we believe that a great pizza starts with high-quality ingredients and a perfectly baked crust. Our pizzas are made with fresh dough, savory tomato sauce, and a variety of toppings to choose from.

Chicken

Chicken is a staple in any great menu, and at PickUp, we've put our own twist on this classic dish. Our chicken dishes are made with high-quality, juicy chicken that's cooked to perfection, and served with a variety of sides to choose from.

Set Meal

At PickUp, we understand that sometimes you just want to sit back, relax, and enjoy a complete meal without having to think about the details. That's why we offer a range of set meals, each with its own unique flavor and combination of dishes.
